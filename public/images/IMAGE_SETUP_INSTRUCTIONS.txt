===========================================
PRODUCT IMAGES SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
===========================================

To add your product photos:

1. Save your Spermaid+ product photo (the white box with blue text) as:
   → spermaid-product.jpg
   
2. Save your Fertil Max product photo (the white box with blue/pink design) as:
   → fertilmax-product.jpg

3. Place both files in this folder:
   → public/images/

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- File names must be exactly as shown above (case-sensitive)
- Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp
- Recommended size: 600x600 pixels or larger
- The images will automatically appear on the website once saved in this folder

After saving the images, refresh your browser at http://localhost:3000
